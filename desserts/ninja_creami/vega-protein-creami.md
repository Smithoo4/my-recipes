# Vega Protein & Greens Ninja Creami "Ice Cream"

A high-protein, veggie-boosted soft-serve using Vega Organic Vanilla Protein & Greens powder as the base.

*ice cream, protein, ninja creami*

**2 servings**

---

- *370 g* Milk (3.25%)
- *40 g* Vega Organic Vanilla Protein & Greens powder
- *15 g* heavy cream (or full-fat coconut milk)
- *12 g* granulated sugar
- *1 g* xanthan gum
- *2 ml* vanilla extract
- *1 pinch* salt

---

Blend the milk, protein powder, cream, sugar, xanthan gum, vanilla extract, and salt together on high until completely smooth, with no clumps of powder.

Pour the blended mixture into a Ninja Creami pint container, staying below the max fill line.

Freeze the pint, lid on, flat on a level shelf for at least 24 hours, until rock solid all the way through.

Place the frozen pint in the Creami machine and run the "Lite Ice Cream" or "Protein Ice Cream" setting.

Check the texture. If it looks crumbly or powdery, add a splash of milk (15-30 g) and run the "Re-spin" function to smooth it out.

Scoop and serve, optionally with mix-ins added before a final quick re-spin.

## Notes

The sugar and xanthan gum aren't just for sweetness — they do real textural work. Sugar lowers the freezing point of the mix, which is what keeps ice cream scoopable instead of freezing into a solid brick; without it, the base freezes harder and turns icier. Xanthan gum traps water into smaller ice crystals, giving a smoother, less grainy mouthfeel; without it, texture tends toward a coarser, "shaved ice" consistency. Both are optional if you'd rather skip them, but expect a firmer, less creamy result and lean more on the re-spin step to compensate.
A pitted medjool date (about 15-18g) can replace the granulated sugar — its natural sugar content does the same freezing-point-lowering job. The key is blending it completely smooth (chop it first, or soak briefly in warm milk to soften) so no fibrous bits are left; undissolved chunks won't lower the freezing point and will just freeze into small chewy or icy flecks instead.
