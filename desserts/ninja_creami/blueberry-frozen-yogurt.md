# Blueberry Frozen Yogurt

Blueberry frozen yogurt tailored for the Ninja Creami. Uses honey or maple syrup
with a splash of vanilla extract to maintain a soft, scoopable texture.

*dessert, frozen yogurt, ninja creami*

**2 Servings**

---

- *150 g* frozen blueberries
- *45 g* honey or pure maple syrup
- *245 g* plain Greek yogurt
- *60 g* whole milk
- *2 g* pure vanilla extract

---

Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.

Pour into the Ninja Creami pint, keeping below the max fill line. Snap on the
lid and freeze on a level surface for 24 hours.

Run warm water around the outside of the pint for 30 seconds, then process on
the LITE ICE CREAM setting.

If crumbly after the first spin, add 1 tablespoon of milk down the center and
press RE-SPIN.
